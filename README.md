## 🛠️ Building now: bitsARK Labs

> Open data infrastructure for Brazil's crypto and FX market. I find where the market lacks clarity and ship the tools to fill the gap. Free, no auth.

`$54B+ stablecoin volume tracked` · `R$50M+ daily FX volume` · `2,000+ alerts delivered` · `24 exchanges indexed` · `100% free`

- **[DolarMap](https://bitsark.com/dolarmap/)**: live Android app tracking real-time USD/BRL across 13 stablecoin exchanges, with rate-move alerts. [Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=dolarmap.app)
- **[Exchanges API](https://bitsark.com/exchanges/api/)**: open, production REST feed for 24 Brazilian exchanges with fiscal and regulatory data (IN 1888, BCB PSAV, CNPJ, Lei 14.754, DeCripto). No keys, no auth. [Code](https://github.com/bitsARK-Labs/exchanges-api)
- **[Stablecoins in Brazil](https://bitsark.com/stablecoins-brasil/)**: research sizing $54B+ on-chain volume across 5+ issuers, updated weekly.
- **[Stablecoin Personas](https://bitsark.com/stablecoins-brasil/personas/)**: 7 user personas mapped from 29 sources.
- **[Exchange Directory](https://bitsark.com/exchanges/)**: 24 entities with CNPJ, tax, and Pix data, plus DeCripto tax reporting.
